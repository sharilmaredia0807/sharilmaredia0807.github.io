01 / About

About

I don't want to build things that only exist on a screen. I want to build hardware that moves - machines that interact with people. There's a special type of challenge and satisfaction in developing hardware humans rely on - and that's my kind of engineering.

I prefer the hard problems - the ones with ambiguous requirements and contradictory constraints. I start with first-principles and embrace the design process, iterating until my math becomes machines and movement. From vehicles, tools, and soon humanoid robots at Sunday, I live for my sketches becoming something real.

When I'm not building cool stuff, I'm probably at the gym or obsessing over the NBA, NFL, or F1 with unhealthy intensity. I’m a two-time fantasy football champion, which is the most important credential on this webpage. As a Texas native, I'm justifiably obsessed with country music and have a very specific devotion to DC Comics.
